Core Economy Commands

Balance

Check your or another user's current balance.

Currency Slash Command
/economy balance [user]

Parameters:

  • user (optional) - The user to check balance for (defaults to yourself)

This command shows the current balance, total earned, and total spent.

Daily Reward

Claim your daily currency reward (once every 24 hours).

Currency Slash Command Cooldown: 24h
/economy daily

Features:

  • Base reward: 200
  • Streak bonus: +10 per consecutive day (max: +100)
  • Keeps track of your streak (resets if you miss a day)

Give Currency

Give some of your currency to another user.

Currency Slash Command
/economy give @user amount

Parameters:

  • user - The user to give money to
  • amount - The amount to give

The recipient will receive a notification when you give them money.

Leaderboard

Display a leaderboard of the richest users in the server.

Currency Slash Command
/economy leaderboard

Shows the top 10 wealthiest users in the server, with special formatting for the top 3 positions. If you're not in the top 10, your rank will be shown at the bottom of the leaderboard.

Shop System

Shop

View items available for purchase in the shop.

Shop Slash Command
/economy shop

Shows all items available in the shop with their prices. Items you can afford will be marked with a ✅.

Available Items:

  • VIP Status (5,000 coins) - Exclusive VIP role in the server
  • Custom Color (2,000 coins) - Change your name color in the server
  • Mystery Lootbox (1,000 coins) - Contains random amount of coins (500-2000)

Buy

Purchase an item from the shop using your currency.

Shop Slash Command
/economy buy item_id

Parameters:

  • item_id - The ID of the item to buy (e.g., vip, colorchange, lootbox)

Different items have different effects when purchased:

  • VIP Status - Gives you the VIP role in the server
  • Custom Color - Prompts you to choose a color for your name
  • Lootbox - Instantly rewards you with a random amount of coins

Gambling Games

Coin Flip

Bet on a coin flip. Double your bet if you win!

Gambling Slash Command
/gamble coinflip amount choice

Parameters:

  • amount - The amount to bet
  • choice - Your guess for the coin flip (heads or tails)

Odds: 50% chance to win
Payout: 2x your bet

Slots

Play the slot machine. Match symbols for different payouts!

Gambling Slash Command
/gamble slots amount

Parameters:

  • amount - The amount to bet

Payouts:

  • Three 7️⃣: 50x your bet
  • Three 💎: 25x your bet
  • Three 🍓 or 🍉: 10x your bet
  • Three 🍊 or 🍇: 5x your bet
  • Three of any other fruit: 3x your bet
  • Two matching symbols: 1-7x your bet (depends on symbol)
